A exclusive matrimony portal for the Indian JAIN Community.

The matrimony.bhartiyajainmilan.com is a part of an religious organization named Bhartiya Jain Milan which is spreaded all over india.

Bhartiya Jain Milan is the most trusted organization since 21st June 1953. It's first unit is established at sangam of holy rivers in Allahabad.

This organization is a unit of diffrent branches of jain religion (Digamber, Shavetamber, Sthanakvasi and Terahpanthi).
